Read the book to know the travails of poor man who faced physical, geographical, financial disability. To his woes was added persecution from family and friends because he dared to make a choice about the religion he wanted to follow. FAQ

  1. How do I read an eBook bought from Amazon?
    Install Amazon Kindle app on your phone or laptop/Desktop PC. Log in using your Amazon username and password. Download the purchased eBook to your phone from within the app. You can also read other eBooks you purchase with that app. Alternatively, if you own a Kindle Reader, you may download the purchased book into the Reader.
  2. How do I read an eBook bought from Google Play Books?
    Install Google Play Books app on your phone or laptop. Log in using your Google username and password. Download the purchased eBook to your phone from within the app.
  3. How do I read free eBooks on my phone?

  4. How do I read eBooks on my computer?
